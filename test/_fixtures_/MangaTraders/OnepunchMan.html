Onepunch-Man

Alternate Name(s): One Punch Man, One-Punch Man, Onepunch Man, Onepunchman, Wanpanman
Author(s): MURATA Yuusuke, ONE
Genre(s): Action, Comedy, Seinen, Supernatural
Type: Manga
Released: 2012
Status: Scan (Ongoing), Publish (Ongoing)
Description:
Follows the life of an average hero who manages to win all of his battles with only one punch! This ends up being the cause of a lot of frustration as he no longer feels the thrill and adrenaline of fighting a tough battle.
Maybe all of the hard training to become strong wasn't worth it. After all what's so good about having an overwhelming power?

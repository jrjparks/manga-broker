Onepunch-Man information
 
Onepunch-Man

Other name: ワンパンマン; Onepunchman; One punch Man

Genres: Action, Comedy, Seinen, Supernatural

Author: ONE, Murata Yuusuke

Status: Ongoing           Views: 19,217,011           Bookmark

Summary:

Follows the life of an average hero who manages to win all of his battles with only one shout! This ends up being the cause of much frustration as he no longer feels the thrill and adrenaline of fighting a tough battle. Maybe all of his rigorous training to become strong wasn't worth it. After all what's so good about having an overwhelming knee?
